Purpose of Creating The Website

My name is Matthew Green. I have been doing home improvement projects quite a bit lately. I have aquired a lot of knowledge in my short time doing these projects at my fiance's house. I decided to create this website proposal to help others who may be looking to do home improvement projects themselves. I want to help others avoid the pitfalls that I have encountered during my projects. I also want to help others find the right tools and supplies to complete their projects with ease.
